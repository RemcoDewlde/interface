PriceCard Maker Manual:


Home screen to editor

Step 1: Use Chrome

To use some of the features of this application you will need a Chrome based browser (Chrome, Opera or Brave)

Step 2: Choose the correct printer and color

On the right side of the print pop-up you will find some settings to choose the correct printer please do this. you will also find the possibility to print in color or in black and white

Step 3: Correct margins

At the bottom of the previously mentioned pop-up you will find the advanced settings, open it. Once opened you will find the setting to change the margins. Set the margins to "None"

Step 4: Branding

Underneath the previously mentioned margins settings you will also find the setting to enable background-graphics. Enable this, otherwise you won't have any branding / logo on your printed priceCard
